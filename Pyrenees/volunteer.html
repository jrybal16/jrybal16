Welcome to Colorado Great Pyrenees Rescue.

If you are looking to get involved, we are always looking for volunteers.

There several ways to volunteer depending on what level of involvement your looking for, such as:

  • Volunteering your time with the animals
  • Donating to our great cause
  • Become a foster
  • Help with transportation
Donation Levels
Great Pyrenees Rescue Donation Amount Details Benefits
Purple $100 Helps us afford quality food for our animals. You will be on our website as a donor and will recieve a picture of the dog you helped. And you will get our annual Great Pyrenees Rescue calendar.
Green $200 Help's us keep the lights on at our facility, and provide shelter for our animals. You will be on our website as a donor and will recieve a picture of the dog you helped. And you will get our annual Great Pyrenees Rescue calendar. Plus get a custom leash and collar from GPR.
White $300 This helps pay for our staff to ensure we have enough people to take care of all the animals coming and going. You will be on our website as a donor and will recieve a picture of the dog you helped. And you will get our annual Great Pyrenees Rescue calendar. Plus get a custom leash and collar from GPR. Put on a plaque in our facility for everyone to see.
Silver $400 Help us pay for transportation costs and the cost of our facility. You will be on our website as a donor and will recieve a picture of the dog you helped. And you will get our annual Great Pyrenees Rescue calendar. Plus get a custom leash and collar from GPR. Put on a plaque in our facility for everyone to see. Will send you a complimentary GPR logo t-shirt and hoodie.
Gold $500 This tier helps us pay for medical issues and urgent care. You will be on our website as a donor and will recieve a picture of the dog you helped. And you will get our annual Great Pyrenees Rescue calendar. Plus get a custom leash and collar from GPR. Put on a plaque in our facility for everyone to see. Will send you a complimentary GPR logo t-shirt and hoodie. Get to come to our Gold tier gala held annually
