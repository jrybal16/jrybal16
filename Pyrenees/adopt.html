Welcome to Colorado Great Pyrenees Rescue.

We always have furry friends looking for new homes, with their forever family.

But first we have a few steps before you can go home with your new friend.

We do an in person interview so we can get to know you, and give you a chance to meet our rescues. We then schedule an at home interview to make sure you have secured yard,and are able to care for these breed, and to make sure the dog is comfortable.

After all that you get to take your new friend home!!!!
