Welcome to Colorado Great Pyrenees Rescue.

We are here to provide resources to Great Pyrenees in Colorado.

We are a non profit organization aiming to educate the public about the Great Pyrenees breed. Theyare extremely cute as puppies, but can quickly be a handful to take care of. A lot of people give up Great Pyrenees after 8 months to 16 months due to their giant size and stubborn behavior.
